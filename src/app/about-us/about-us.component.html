About Us

The Team

The Charity Connect team is a small group of insurance professionals on the road to becoming professional developers. Team Members include: Britta Mielke, David Hall, Lisa Forrest, and Beth Skwerski.

The Project

The Charity Connect project is a final class project for Java boot camp. This project leverages Java, PostgreSQL, Angular, Amazon AWS S3 storage, and Bootstrap.
